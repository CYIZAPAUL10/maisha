Technology Solutions

Tools that give
voice to the voiceless

We deploy a suite of inclusive digital tools combining AAC technology, AI, and accessible mental health platforms.

🎙️

AAC Devices & Apps

Augmentative and Alternative Communication tools like Pocket Go-Talk 5-Level Communication Devices help non-verbal users express themselves effectively.

🤖

AI Sign Language Translator

Camera-based AI that detects sign language and converts it instantly to text or speech — enabling seamless communication between deaf and hearing individuals.

🧠

Mental Health Platforms

Accessible mental health platforms with easy-to-use UI, visual and audio therapy tools specifically designed for persons with disabilities.

📝

Speech-to-Text & TTS

AI-powered apps that convert speech to text and text to speech, enabling non-verbal users to communicate in both directions with confidence.

🤝

Virtual Peer Support

Moderated virtual peer support groups where individuals can connect, share experiences, and receive mental wellness guidance from trained facilitators.

🛠️

Local AAC Innovation

Developing locally-adapted AAC tools that are affordable and contextually relevant, ensuring access for communities with limited resources.
